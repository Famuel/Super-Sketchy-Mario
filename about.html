Welcome to Super Sketchy Mario



Super Sketchy Mario is a video game based, comedic, webcomic focused on the worlds of many different video game franchises (with heavy influences from Brawl in the Family). While the namesake of the comic and the most common characters you'll be seeing are Mario characters, many other franchises both big and small are represented here. If you're a fan of A Hat in Time, Tomodachi Life, and Toontown among others, then I'm sure you'll find something to enjoy here. If you're looking for something new, the I've got you covered. SSM has no shortage of strange made-up characters including the strange trio of Personalities, treasure hunter Jones of the Mushroom Kingdom, and the straight-man and funny-guy duo of Westhaven: Hannah and Derrick. Weird things happen every day in this strange comic world. Maybe a samurai king is trying to learn about Italy, a purple-clad tennis master is making his home in a bush, or a haunted robot is making sandwiches that no reasonable person would touch. It's a peck of a time that I hope you'll stick around for!

Upload Schedule: I try to upload new comics here and on DeviantArt every weekend, usually in the early to mid-afternoon.

This website is also home to SSM Art, a collection of various drawings I have either made myself, or some of my amazingly talented friends have made for me (uploaded with their permission, of course). Unlike Super Sketchy Mario, these drawing have no set upload schedule and more or less come out when I feel like it. Check those out under the Art section!

The characters I own are Comic-Sans Mario characters, the Personalities, SSM Originals characters, Jones, Prince Ernie Ruffleton and Googletoon, Tomodachi Life is Weird's Jamie and Gavin, Chuckle Elementary's Bobby, Sebastian, and Bernie, and a few inconsequential one-off characters.
