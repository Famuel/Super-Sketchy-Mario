Princess Luigi

Based on that one panel from the Mario and Luigi Superstar Saga + Bowser's Minions review. The moment in Superstar Saga when the bros trick Bowletta and Fawful into thinking Luigi is Peach is my absolute favorite moment in video games, and it only got better with the remake! I absolutely love the addition of the paint cans in the remake because it really makes sense: Luigi is shorter than Peach so he'd need something to make him look taller. I guess since I brought up that review comic I can finally say that, after some thought, it doesn't reflect my real opinion on the game. Honestly, I'd give it a 12/10 as opposed to the original 13/10. I might talk about why in the future, but now's not the future (I mean, you are seeing this after I post it... so...).
